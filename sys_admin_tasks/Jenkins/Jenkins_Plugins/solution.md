![](./img/1.png)

2. Once logged in, click on `Manage Jenkins`

![](./img/2.png)

3. Choose `Manage Plugins`

![](./img/3.png)

4. Under `Available Plugins`, Choose `Git` and `GitLab`

![](./img/4.png)

![](./img/5.png)

5. Then, install plugins

![](./img/6.png)
